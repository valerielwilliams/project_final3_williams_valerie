About Us

About Us

Hi, I'm Bob. Bob's Burgers is a family-owned restaurant that has specialized in one thing since 1997: cooking burgers. All of our burgers are 100% Grade A beef, grilled with care by me and served by my wife and kids.

I love my family, but they're terrible at what they do here, and I feel like I should tell you that I'd fire them all if I could.
